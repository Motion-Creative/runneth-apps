## Super powers this unlocks

- Go from sentence to production-ready ad in one conversation.
- Keep the brand inside the prompt automatically.
- Review the prompt before any image generates.
- Get a properly named ad ready for staging.

## How it works

Describe what you want. Your Runneth writes the brief, refines the image prompt against your brand kit, and asks you to approve before generating. The image comes back named and ready to push to Meta.

## A real example

Faye is one ad short on a Friday afternoon. She types: hero static for the new SKU, kitchen scene, lifestyle, focus on the product label. Her Runneth writes the brief, comes back with a refined prompt for review, and waits. She tweaks one line and approves. Sixty seconds later she has a clean image, a proper ad name, and a ready-to-stage file in her downloads.
