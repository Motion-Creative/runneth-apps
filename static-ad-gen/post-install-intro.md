# Static ad generator

## What just opened up
You can now generate production-ready static ads from chat. Say "generate an ad for [product]" and Runneth pulls the product's performance data and prior learnings, derives the persona and messaging angle, builds a structured brief for your review, then on approval refines the prompt with Gemini, generates the image with NB2, downloads it, and writes a production ad name. End-to-end without leaving the conversation.

## Try this now
1. **Generate your first ad**: `Generate an ad for [product name].`
   _You'll get back:_ performance pull, persona and angle derived, a structured brief surfaced for review. On approval, the refined prompt, generated image, and production ad name.
2. **Add a new product to the catalog**: `Set up the static ad generator for a new product.`
   _You'll get back:_ the setup skill walks brand voice, reference images, audience persona, and saves the product to the catalog ready to generate against.
3. **Iterate on a concept**: `Generate three variants of that ad with different hooks.`
   _You'll get back:_ three briefs sharing the visual frame but testing different hook angles, each reviewable before generation.

## Compounds with
- **brand-audit:** The audit's persona and messaging layers feed every brief.
- **creative-deep-dive:** Diagnostic feedback on generated ads improves the next round.
