# Competitor intel

## What just opened up
You now have a weekly competitive read that lands in Slack without anyone asking for it. Every Monday morning, Runneth pulls each watchlisted brand's active ads, runs survival cohort analysis, diffs against last week, and delivers a delta-first report. The value is in the delta: what's new, what survived, what they killed.

## Try this now
1. **Run a scan right now**: `Run a competitor scan.`
   _You'll get back:_ a fresh report covering all watchlisted brands. First run establishes baseline; second run is when the deltas start.
2. **Add a brand mid-week**: `Add [brand name] to the competitor watch.`
   _You'll get back:_ that brand resolved in Motion's ad library, added to the weekly routine, and saved to `/agent/brain/competition/{{WORKSPACE_SLUG}}/inspoBrands.json`.
3. **Ask a one-off question about a watched brand**: `What's [brand name] been testing lately?`
   _You'll get back:_ their recent test clusters, hook patterns, and what's surviving past 14 days.

- **brand-audit:** Cross-references competitor positioning against your own brand framework.
