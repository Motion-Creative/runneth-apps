# Competitor intel

## What just opened up
Runneth can now scan your competitors' ads whenever you ask. The first time, it asks which 3–5 brands to watch. Every scan after that pulls each brand's live ads from Motion's ad library, tells you in chat what they're running and what changed since the last scan, and keeps the summary in a durable watch file so later competitor questions answer from what's already known. Nothing runs on a schedule and nothing posts anywhere — if you want a recurring scan, just ask Runneth to set one up.

## Try this now
1. **Run your first scan**: `What are my competitors running?`
   _You'll get back:_ a quick question about which brands to track, then a per-brand summary of their live ads. The first scan records the baseline; the next one starts showing what changed.
2. **Re-scan later**: `Run a competitor scan.`
   _You'll get back:_ what's new, what's gone, and what shifted per brand since the last scan date.
3. **Ask about one brand**: `What's [brand name] been testing lately?`
   _You'll get back:_ their recent angles and verbatim hooks, answered from the watch file and refreshed if it's stale.

## Compounds with
- **weekly-performance-deck:** Run a scan before building the deck so the team sees market context next to results.
- **brand-audit:** Cross-references competitor positioning against your own brand framework.
