# Motion 2026 Creative Strategy Bootcamp — Syllabus

8-week live training program. All Tuesday classes at 1 PM EST, followed by Thursday group coaching at 1 PM EST. Recordings available within 24 hours.

**Certification requirements:**
- Attend 5+ live classes or watch replays (60+ minutes each)
- Score 70%+ on final certification exam
- Homework and coaching sessions optional but encouraged

---

## Week 1 — What is Creative Strategy?
- **Tuesday class** — Evan Lee. "How to run an effective creative diagnosis at any brand." Foundation of creative strategy and the science behind effective hooks.
- **Thursday group coaching** — "How I write hooks + group coaching." Creative strategists share personal hook-writing techniques; live work reviews.
- **Homework**: post in `#homework-week-1` by Friday.

## Week 2 — Research Your Ad Ideas
- **Tuesday class** — Sarah Levinger. Psychology-based customer research methods. AI-native creative strategy research tools. 90-minute in-depth research techniques. Insights as foundation for strong creative.
- **Thursday group coaching** — "How I research ad ideas + group coaching."
- **Homework**: post in `#homework-week-2` by Friday.

## Week 3 — Prioritize Your Ad Ideas
- **Tuesday class** — Dara Denney. Pressure-testing creative concepts. Creative strategy decision trees. Organizing research into actionable creative bets. Selecting high-potential ideas and validating before production.
- **Thursday group coaching** — "How I run a monthly creative retro + group coaching." Guest: Naomi Peh Haeger (Creative Strategist, Spacegoods) shares monthly creative retrospective process.
- **Homework**: post in `#homework-week-3` by Friday.

## Week 4 — Make Some Ads!
- **Tuesday class** — Will Sartorius. AI-assisted ad production without sacrificing quality. Prompt iteration loops for generative control. Static product ads, animated GIFs, production-ready B-roll. Balancing AI efficiency with human creative control.
- **Thursday group coaching** — "How I write scripts, storyboards, and briefs + group coaching."
- **Homework**: post in `#homework-week-4` by Friday.

## Week 5 — Analyze Your Ads
- **Tuesday class** — Evan Lee. Proper creative analysis methodology. AI-assisted measurement and diagnostics. Diagnosing why creative succeeds or fails. Data-driven creative iteration.
- **Thursday group coaching** — "How I use AI for analysis, research, and ideation." Motion engineers and strategists share AI toolboxes.
- **Homework**: post in `#homework-week-5` by Friday.

## Week 6 — Sprint #1: Iterate on Your Ads
- **Theme**: "Your first ad didn't work — now what?"
- **Tuesday class** — Evan Lee + creative strategy coaches. Real-world iteration under feedback. Fast decision-making with imperfect inputs. Learning from previous ad performance. Guest speakers: Emily Spring (DROOL), Daniel Rivera (Harry's), Jade Heritage (Calm) share "Winning Ad Stories."
- **Thursday group coaching** — Darcy Tennant (Heights, formerly Flo) on onboarding strategies for new creative roles; agency-side perspective.
- **Homework**: complete iteration sprint, post in `#homework-week-6` by Friday.

## Week 7 — Sprint #2: Adapt Winning Ads Into New Formats
- **Theme**: "This ad works — how do you scale it?"
- **Tuesday class** — Evan Lee + creative strategy coaches. Multi-format adaptation of successful ads. Production scaling techniques. UGC creator integration. Guest speakers: Viti Videtta (Happy Mammoth) on rapid production delivery, Sophia Beauvoir (Ready Set) on large-agency scaling, Janae LeVander (Caraway) on UGC scaling.
- **Homework**: scaling sprint assignment in `#homework-week-7` by Friday.

## Week 8 — Sprint #3: Take a Big Swing!
- **Theme**: "Let's unlock a new audience with a big winning concept."
- **Tuesday class** — Evan Lee + creative strategy coaches. Capstone creative project. Data-driven narrative storytelling. Client presentation and insight communication. Guest: Cate Wright (Director of Creative Strategy, Monks) on "the art of shaping a narrative."
- **Homework**: produce script + storyboard with innovative concept; submit in `#homework-week-8` by Friday.

---

## Weeks 9-10: The Master's Series (Optional)

Pattern-exposure lectures; not required for certification.

- **Session 1: The Viral Ad Playbook** — Oren John. 90-minute workshop on blending organic social trends into paid creative. Personal research workflows; cultural moment integration.
- **Session 2: Scaling Baseball Lifestyle from 8 to 9 Figures** — Bill Rom (Co-founder, Baseball Lifestyle 101). Growth levers: organic social, top-of-funnel, profitable acquisition. Case study: scaling from 5K to 1M+ followers and 100+ employees.
