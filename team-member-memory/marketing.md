## Super powers this unlocks

- Knows who's talking before you say a word.
- Picks up where the last chat left off.
- Saves new preferences the moment they come up.
- Writes a one-pager per chat so anyone can catch up in 30 seconds.

## How it works

Every chat starts with your Runneth reading the file on you and the last one-pager from a past session. Every chat ends with it updating both, so the next one starts smarter.

## A real example

Sophia opens Runneth on a Monday. Without saying who she is, it surfaces the two accounts that went cold last week and asks if she wants the one-line summary she usually prefers. The next day, when she pivots to drafting a creative QA template, Runneth opens with examples instead of the framework. By Friday the file on Sophia is sharper than what a new hire would write after a month.
