## Super powers this unlocks

- Opens every chat already knowing the person's role, priorities, and working style.
- Picks up where the last conversation left off, on Slack or Motion web.
- Updates its picture of each person every session, without being asked.
- Writes a one-pager after every chat so anyone can catch up in 30 seconds.

## How it works

At the start of every conversation, your Runneth resolves who's talking using the permissions system, reads their team file, and loads their most recent conversation recap. At the end, it updates both. The file on each person gets sharper every session.

## A real example

Kyra opens Runneth on a Monday. Without saying who she is, it surfaces the two accounts that went quiet last week and asks if she wants the one-line summary she usually prefers. The next day, when she pivots to a creative QA template, Runneth opens with examples instead of a framework. Works the same way when she tags it in Slack.
