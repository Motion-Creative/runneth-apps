## Super powers this unlocks

- Opens every chat already knowing the person's role, priorities, and working style.
- Picks up where the last conversation left off, on Slack or Motion web.
- Updates its picture of each person every session, without being asked.
- Writes a one-pager after every chat so the whole team stays in sync.

## How it works

At the start of every conversation, your Runneth reads the file on that person and their most recent conversation recap. At the end, it updates both. The file on each person gets sharper every session.

## A real example

A creative strategist at a performance agency opens Runneth on a Monday. Without saying who she is, it surfaces the three ad concepts she was iterating on last week and asks if she wants to pick up where she left off. When she pivots to briefing a new UGC batch, it already knows her preferred brief format. Two weeks in, it feels less like a tool and more like a second brain.
