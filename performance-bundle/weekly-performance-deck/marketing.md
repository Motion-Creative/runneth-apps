## Super powers this unlocks

- A weekly deck that actually continues the story instead of resetting every Friday.
- Brand-styled rendering — colors, type, motion all come from the brand kit, not Motion's defaults.
- Per funnel stage: overview, new launches and active tests, deep dives, recommendations. Every stage gets the same treatment.
- Top creatives and creatives that shifted get the full 5-skill diagnostic. Tests that reached readable spend get test-set deep dives on every creative in the set.
- Dated routes that accumulate. Week 17 doesn't overwrite week 16. You can scroll back through 12 months of decks at their original URLs.
- Slack-shareable app URLs anyone can open.

## How it works

Every Friday at 2pm local, Runneth reads the workspace's strategy brief(s), reads last Friday's snapshot, pulls the last 7 days of data per channel, and identifies what's stable, what changed, what's new, and what got resolved this week.

For top creatives and shifted creatives in every funnel stage, Runneth runs creative-deep-dive. For tests that reached readable spend, it runs creative-deep-dive in test-set mode on every creative in the set so the recommendations are sharp.

Then it renders an HTML deck — brand-styled — at a new dated route on the workspace's deck app, posts the share URL to your configured Slack channels, and writes a snapshot file so next Friday has something to frame against.

## A real example

Week 4 deck opens with the cover slide noting "Spend up 8% on TikTok, flat on Meta — top performers held, two new tests in flight, last week's hook-iteration recommendation now scaling."

The Meta Prospecting section overview slide reads "Prospecting held the line. Top 3 unchanged; $/event drifted from $89 to $86, well inside the band."

The active-tests slide notes "Last week we recommended testing whether the panic-window framing or the 'like usual' normalization was carrying the FUN34166 hook. Variant FUN34170-IT-R1 launched Tuesday. Early read: panic-window framing carrying — variant 2 already at $74/event."

The recommendations slide for the next week: "Ship variant 3 with non-5pm daypart, hold panic-window framing constant. Tests the daypart sensitivity independently."

That's the level of continuity the deck is built to deliver.
