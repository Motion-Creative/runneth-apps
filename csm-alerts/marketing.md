## Super powers this unlocks

- Flags gone-dark accounts every Monday morning.
- Catches cliff drops and three-week fading trends.
- Watches for champion shifts before retention conversations start.
- Lands the digest in your team channel automatically.

## How it works

Every Monday morning your Runneth scans your book of business, compares week-over-week usage against the prior pattern, and posts the at-risk accounts to your channel with the specific signal that fired.

## A real example

Josh opens Slack Monday morning. The csm-alerts post lists three accounts: one gone-dark for two weeks, one whose champion was last active six weeks ago, one whose usage is 60% off the prior month's pace. Two were on his radar. The third surprised him. He pulls the champion onto a call the same day and saves the relationship before the renewal conversation.
