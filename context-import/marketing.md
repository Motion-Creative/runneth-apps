## Super powers this unlocks

- Pull everything ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini knows about you in one paste.
- Catch conflicts before they overwrite what Runneth already learned about you.
- Promote anything from personal to team-wide with one word in chat.
- Leave the noise behind. One-off debug chats never make it into the brain.

## How it works

Runneth hands you a prompt. You paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. That AI dumps everything it knows about you into one file. You upload it back, review what Runneth flagged, say go.

## A real example

A creative strategist at a DTC skincare brand. Two years on ChatGPT. Custom instructions about her tone. Project context for six brands. A signature brief format she'd taught it across hundreds of chats. One paste, twelve minutes. Runneth handed back a review with 73 items sorted by category and three flagged as conflicting with what it had already learned about her. She approved 64, promoted her brand voice doc to the team, skipped the rest. Cold to caught-up before lunch.
