## Super powers this unlocks

- Click-to-pin comments on statics.
- Timestamped notes on videos.
- Auto-routes objective issues to the team and judgment calls to the strategist.
- Gets sharper at your standards every time you accept or reject a flag.

## How it works

Drop an ad into the app. Your Runneth reviews it for the issues it has seen before, places its comments where they belong, and asks you whether each one was right. Your responses train a personal judge stored in your brain.

## A real example

Reza uploads four new statics on a Thursday morning. Within a minute the app shows pin comments: one CTA is too low on the page, one headline doesn't match the landing page, two are clean. He accepts the first, rejects the second after checking the page. By the next batch the judge has stopped raising mismatches Reza doesn't agree with, and the review takes half as long.
