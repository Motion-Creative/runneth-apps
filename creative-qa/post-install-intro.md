# Creative QA

## What just opened up
You now have a creative review app where Runneth scores uploaded ads and trains a personal QA judge from your accepts and rejects. Static images get click-to-annotate pin comments, videos get timestamp-anchored comments, and a monitor routine runs the QA pass automatically within minutes of an upload.

## Try this now
1. **Upload an ad and read the QA**: `Open creative-qa.`
   _You'll get back:_ the app opens; drop in a static or video ad and Runneth posts its QA verdict, scoring rationale, and routing recommendation (Objective vs. Subjective track).
2. **Train the judge with one accept or reject**: `On the last QA, accept the feedback.`
   _You'll get back:_ the accept logged to your training history, the judge's rubric updated, future reviews adjust toward that signal.
3. **See how the judge is evolving**: `What's the current QA rubric and how has it shifted in the last 10 reviews?`
   _You'll get back:_ the live rubric with the deltas it's learned from your training signal.

## Compounds with
