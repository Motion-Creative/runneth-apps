# Competitor Watch — Marketplace Listing

**Tagline:** Weekly competitive intelligence — what changed, not just what exists

**Category:** Competitive Intelligence

**Pitch (2–3 sentences):**
Stop manually pulling competitor ads. Competitor Watch scans your tracked brands every Monday,
runs survival analysis on their full ad portfolios, diffs against last week's baseline, and posts
a delta-first intelligence report to your Slack channel automatically. The second run is where it
gets useful — you'll see exactly what they launched, what survived, and what they stopped doing.

**What it enables:**
- Know when a competitor makes a meaningful creative or messaging move — before your next planning meeting
- See which of their ads are actually working (survival signals) vs which are fresh bets
- Spot messaging angles you're missing and feature claims worth countering
- Get specific recommendations grounded in what competitors are actually doing in market

**Who it's for:**
Creative strategists and performance marketing leads who want competitive intelligence without
spending hours pulling and analyzing ads manually.

**Setup time:** ~2 minutes

**Runs:** Weekly on schedule (default Monday 9am) + on explicit request

**Requires:** Motion workspace with competitor/inspo brands accessible in the ad library

**Depends on:** Nothing — standalone use case
