## Super powers this unlocks

- Picks the right depth based on who's setting it up.
- Solves the platform quirks proactively, not after you hit them.
- Writes a thin CLI shaped by how your team actually uses the tool.
- Leaves behind a runbook the next person can read.

## How it works

When an integration connects, your Runneth detects whether this is a quick pull or a team-wide setup, runs the matching protocol, and verifies live before handing off. Common quirks are surfaced as solved, not as bugs.

## A real example

Faye connects Apollo for the first time. Her Runneth notices it's a fresh setup, runs the full protocol, validates the auth, builds a thin CLI that wraps the calls she actually needs, and writes a one-page guide. Faye runs her first query five minutes after starting. Two weeks later a teammate sets up the same integration in their sandbox and follows the same guide without asking Faye anything.
