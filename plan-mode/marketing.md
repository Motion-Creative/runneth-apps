## Super powers this unlocks

- Asks the questions that would actually change the architecture.
- Hands you a plan file you can open, edit, or share.
- Won't touch a routine, skill, or app until you say go.
- Catches the wrong direction before it costs you a rebuild.

## How it works

Before your Runneth creates or modifies anything durable, it stops, asks the two to four questions where the answer would change the design, and writes the spec as a markdown plan file. Building only starts after you approve.

## A real example

Reza asks his Runneth for a new winners-of-the-week routine. Before touching anything, it asks four questions: which workspace, what counts as the week, whether "winner" means top spend or top ROAS, and where the digest should land. Reza answers in two lines. The plan comes back with the trigger, the storage layer, and the Slack target spelled out. He approves, and the routine is live thirty seconds later.
