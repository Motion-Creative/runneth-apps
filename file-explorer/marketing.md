## Super powers this unlocks

- See exactly what your Runneth has saved.
- Open, preview, and edit files without leaving the app.
- Spot stale files and dead routines at a glance.
- Confirm a save landed where you expected.

## How it works

The file explorer runs as a sandbox app that reads the live filesystem. You navigate the tree, preview the contents, and edit in place. What you see is what your Runneth sees.

## A real example

Faye asks her Runneth to save a new routine and wants to confirm it landed where she expected. She opens the file explorer, navigates to the routines folder, and sees the file at the top with today's timestamp. While she's there she spots an old prototype, opens it, and deletes it in place. The whole audit takes a minute.
