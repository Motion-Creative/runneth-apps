## Super powers this unlocks

- Real customer words for hooks, angles, and landing page copy.
- Pulls from any platform with a JSON endpoint.
- Tags every review by theme so you can search by what you need.
- Keeps itself current with a weekly sync.

## How it works

Once a week your Runneth pulls new reviews from the platform you point it at and saves the high-signal ones to your brain, tagged by theme: pain, win, objection, novelty. Ask for hook ideas and your Runneth has real customers' words to quote, not guesses.

## A real example

Faye is writing the launch ad for a new SKU. She asks Runneth for three hooks built around "this product solved a problem I'd given up on." It quotes six reviews tagged "win" and writes the three hooks underneath. Two ship that afternoon. One outperforms the previous best ad inside a week.
