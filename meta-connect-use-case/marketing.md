## Super powers this unlocks

- Say "push these to Meta as a new ad set" and your Runneth does it.
- Stages everything as drafts so nothing goes live without you.
- Stays connected for 60 days at a time, no re-auth pop-ups.
- Ship without opening Ads Manager outside of approval.

## How it works

Once connected, your Runneth holds an OAuth token that auto-refreshes. Drop assets into chat, name the campaign, and it builds the ad set and creative records as drafts you activate in Ads Manager.

## A real example

Reza is reviewing five new statics on a Tuesday afternoon. He drops them into chat: stage in the Q2 acquisition campaign as a new ad set, mirror the targeting from the top performer. Three minutes later he is in Ads Manager looking at five draft ads, correctly named, in the right ad set. He activates and moves on. The handoff takes less time than approving the assets did.
