## Super powers this unlocks

- Catches the gaps a tired strategist would miss.
- Flags vague language and missing creative inputs.
- Learns your taste from your accept and reject signals.
- Becomes specifically yours after a couple of weeks.

## How it works

Save a brief to the app. Your Runneth reviews it against the patterns it has seen work, surfaces the issues, and asks you whether each call was right. Your accepts and rejects feed back into a personal QA judge stored in your brain.

## A real example

Faye writes a brief on Tuesday afternoon and saves it to the app. Within a minute her Runneth flags four things: an audience that's too broad, a hook that contradicts the page, no examples of the desired ad style, and a missing success metric. She accepts three, rejects one. Two weeks later the judge stops bringing up the kind of thing she always rejects, and the briefs need less fixing.
