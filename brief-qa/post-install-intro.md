# Brief QA

## What just opened up
You now have a brief review app where Runneth scores saved briefs against a rubric and trains a personal QA judge from your accepts and rejects. Save a brief to the app, get a scored review with specific feedback, accept or reject the call, and the judge gets sharper for next time.

## Try this now
1. **Open the app and save your first brief**: `Open brief-qa.`
   _You'll get back:_ the app opens; paste or import a brief and Runneth posts its scored review with rubric breakdown and rewrite suggestions.
2. **Train the judge**: `Accept the rewrite suggestion on that brief.`
   _You'll get back:_ training entry logged, the judge's rubric adapts, future briefs scored with that signal applied.
3. **See score trends across your saved briefs**: `What's my brief quality looking like over the last month?`
   _You'll get back:_ score history, common weak spots, and the rubric dimensions trending up or down.

## Compounds with
- **briefing skill:** Briefs produced by Runneth's briefing flow can be saved directly to brief-qa for review.
