## Super powers this unlocks

- Scans every routine for storage drift.
- Tells you exactly which files moved out of bounds.
- Suggests the right home for each misplaced file.
- Catches dead routines that are still writing.

## How it works

Your Runneth walks every routine, follows where it actually writes, and compares that against the canonical home for that kind of file. Anything misplaced gets flagged with a one-line fix.

## A real example

Sophia is six weeks in and her brain is starting to feel cluttered. She asks for a storage audit. Three minutes later she has a list: a customer note saved into the templates folder, a brief saved into conversations, two routines still writing to a deprecated path. She accepts the suggested moves and her brain is back to clean. Nothing lost, nothing renamed without her say-so.
