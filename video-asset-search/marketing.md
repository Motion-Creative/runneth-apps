## Super powers this unlocks

- Search by what's on screen, not just the filename.
- Get back deep-linked clips, not whole videos.
- Copy timecodes, download, or open source in one click.
- Stay inside the library you already have.

## How it works

Your Runneth indexes every video in the library with scene-level descriptions. Queries like "woman holding the bottle in golden hour light" return the right clip with the timestamp, ranked by match strength.

## A real example

Sophia's editor needs B-roll of hands working with the product in outdoor light. She opens video-asset-search and types exactly that. Four results come back in a second, each with a clickable timecode. She drops the strongest into the edit. The whole round trip is faster than scrolling the library would have been.
