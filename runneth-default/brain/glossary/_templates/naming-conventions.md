# Naming Conventions Template

Use this to seed and confirm how names map to business meaning.

## Seed From

- Recent campaign, ad set, and ad names.
- Motion filter reference.
- Paid strategy brief.
- Customer setup notes.

## Confirm With Team

Show the inferred pattern and ask the team to correct any token meanings.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/naming-conventions.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Pattern.
- Token positions.
- Token meanings.
- Funnel, product, audience, offer, or test mappings.
- Known exceptions.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
