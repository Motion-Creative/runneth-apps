# Channel Roles Template

Use this to seed and confirm what each connected channel is for.

## Seed From

- Connected integrations.
- Paid strategy briefs.
- Cross-channel strategy notes.
- Customer setup notes.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether the seeded role for each channel is correct.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/channel-roles.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Channel name.
- Role in the mix.
- Primary KPI or KPI override.
- Source of truth.
- Questions that should route to this channel.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
