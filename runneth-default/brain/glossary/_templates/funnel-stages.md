# Funnel Stages Template

Use this to seed and confirm how the team detects funnel stages.

## Seed From

- Naming conventions.
- Campaign objectives.
- Audience setup.
- Paid strategy brief.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether Runneth should split the funnel using the seeded stage names and detection rules.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/funnel-stages.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Stage names.
- Detection rules.
- Campaign or audience signals.
- KPI overrides by stage.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
