# Primary KPI Template

Use this to seed and confirm the metric the account should be graded on.

## Seed From

- Motion workspace goal.
- Paid strategy brief when it provides a validated metric mapping.
- Alignment notes or setup calls that name what success means.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether Runneth should grade the account on the seeded metric and direction.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/primary-kpi.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Display name.
- Metric key or source field.
- Direction: higher is better or lower is better.
- Source of truth.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
