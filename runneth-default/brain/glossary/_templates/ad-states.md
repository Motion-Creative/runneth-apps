# Ad States Template

Use this to seed and confirm how the team labels creative lifecycle states.

## Seed From

- Spend threshold.
- Winner definition.
- Naming conventions.
- Customer instructions about testing, scaling, evergreen, paused, or graveyard states.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether the seeded state labels and entry or exit criteria are right.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/ad-states.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- State names.
- Entry criteria.
- Exit criteria.
- Source fields or naming tokens.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
