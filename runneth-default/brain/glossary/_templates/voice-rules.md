# Voice Rules Template

Use this to seed and confirm copy rules that should apply to hooks, briefs, and creative output.

## Seed From

- Motion brand context.
- Brand audit.
- Website or ad copy samples.
- Customer feedback about tone, banned words, or legal guardrails.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether the seeded do, do-not, and banned-word rules are right.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/voice-rules.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Do rules.
- Do-not rules.
- Banned words or claims.
- Required phrases or disclaimers.
- Scope, such as all copy, paid social only, or briefs only.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
