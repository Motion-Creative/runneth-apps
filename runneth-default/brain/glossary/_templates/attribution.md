# Attribution Template

Use this to seed and confirm how conversion reads should be attributed.

## Seed From

- Motion workspace goal attribution windows.
- Paid strategy brief.
- Integration maps for Northbeam, Triple Whale, or another attribution source.
- Customer setup calls or saved reporting preferences.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether conversions should be read from the seeded source and click/view windows.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/attribution.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Source of truth.
- Click attribution window.
- View attribution window.
- Metric mapping.
- Known source discrepancies.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
