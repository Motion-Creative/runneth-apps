# Winner Definition Template

Use this to seed and confirm the bar for calling a creative a winner or loser.

## Seed From

- Paid strategy brief.
- Spend threshold.
- Primary KPI.
- Repeated customer instructions about what counts as a winner.

## Confirm With Team

Ask whether the seeded winner criteria and spend floor are right.

## Write

Create `/agent/brain/glossary/winner-definition.json` or another customer-approved glossary file. Mark it as seeded until confirmed.

## Fields To Capture

- Primary metric.
- Minimum spend or data threshold.
- Winner criteria.
- Loser or watch criteria.
- Exceptions by channel, campaign type, or funnel stage.
- Confidence and confirmation status.
