## Super powers this unlocks

- Catches recommendations that didn't pan out, automatically.
- Improves the next version of a routine without you babysitting it.
- Tags the wins so good calls don't get lost.
- Routes corrections to the right brain layer so they actually stick.

## How it works

Every time your Runneth builds a recurring routine, app, or report, it wires in a feedback loop tailored to that specific output. When the predicted thing happens (or doesn't), your Runneth records the outcome and feeds the lesson back into the next run.

## A real example

Faye's Runneth runs a weekly creative fatigue report. One week it flags two ads as fatigued. By Friday only one of them actually dropped. The loop records the miss, notes what the false-positive looked like, and adjusts the threshold for the next run. Three weeks later the report is at a 90% hit rate. Faye never had to tune it.
