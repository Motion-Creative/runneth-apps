## Super powers this unlocks

- Pull everything ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini knows about you in one paste.
- Your tone, voice, and format preferences load at every session, no re-teaching.
- Project context, decisions, and memories stay searchable when you need them.
- Raise anything from personal to team-wide with one word in chat.
- Leave the noise behind. One-off debug chats never make it into the brain.

## How it works

Runneth hands you a prompt. You paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. That AI dumps everything it knows about you into one file. You upload it back, review what Runneth flagged, say go.

## A real example

A creative strategist at a DTC skincare brand. Two years on ChatGPT. Custom instructions about her tone. Project context for six brands. A signature brief format she'd taught it across hundreds of chats. One paste, twelve minutes. Runneth handed back a review with 73 items: 12 behavioral preferences ready to load every session, 52 contextual items going to imports, 9 noise. She approved everything but the noise, raised her brand voice doc to the team, said go. Next session her Runneth opened already knowing her tone, her brief shape, and which six brands she manages. Cold to caught-up before lunch.
