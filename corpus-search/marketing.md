## Super powers this unlocks

- Search by meaning, not exact words.
- Indexes video summaries, call transcripts, briefs, notes.
- Sub-second results against tens of thousands of chunks.
- Lives in your sandbox. No server. No data leaves.

## How it works

Point your Runneth at a folder of markdown, run index once, and from then on every search uses keyword and semantic together, ranked. Queries like "the moment the founder admits the original price was too low" return the exact chunk with a timestamp.

## A real example

Josh's customer ships UGC every Friday. By month three the library is 400 clips. He asks his Runneth to find every clip where the creator pulls the product out of the package without looking at the camera. Five results come back in under a second, each with a timestamped link. He drops the strongest into the new ad brief without opening a single video.
