## Super powers this unlocks

- Ten to twenty tests, ready to queue.
- Variant copy that already sounds on-brand.
- Scoring by expected lift, effort, and win rate.
- Names the failure mode for each test before you ship it.

## How it works

Your Runneth pulls a fresh page summary, reads your brand kit so variants sound on-brand, and reads prior audits so it doesn't re-suggest fixes already shipped. The output is a scored backlog with primary metrics, guardrails, sample size estimates, and a "why this might lose" note per test.

## A real example

Sophia's customer wants a full Q3 testing roadmap. She asks her Runneth for an experiment plan on their main acquisition page. Fifteen minutes later she has fourteen tests, scored and grouped, with on-brand variant copy already drafted. She walks the customer through the top five on the next call. Three are in flight inside the week.
