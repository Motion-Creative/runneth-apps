## Super powers this unlocks

- Captures a page deep enough to rebuild from the spec.
- Names the sections the way a strategist would.
- Reads the voice, not just the words.
- Becomes the evidence layer for every audit, kit, or experiment that follows.

## How it works

Your Runneth visits the page in a real browser, walks every section, and writes a structured markdown spec to a stable path. Downstream skills (brand-kit, optimize-landing-page) read from the same file.

## A real example

Sophia is auditing a competitor's new landing page on a Monday morning. She asks her Runneth to summarize it. Sixty seconds later she has the hero copy, every CTA, the form fields, the section logic, and the voice rules, all in one markdown file. She drops the file into the brief for her team's response without rewriting a thing.
