## Super powers this unlocks

- Builds a complete kit from a URL in under a minute.
- Captures voice and visual rules deep enough to act on.

## How it works

Point your Runneth at a URL and it does a deep read in a real browser, pulling colors, typography, and voice from the live site. From then on, every branded output you ask for auto-consults the kit.

Sophia inherits a new customer halfway through a campaign. She tells her Runneth to build a brand kit. Sixty seconds later she has the colors, the voice rules, the things this brand never says, and three example headlines that sound like them. The next ad brief opens with the customer's actual tone. By week two, Runneth is shipping on-brand variants without her thinking about it.
