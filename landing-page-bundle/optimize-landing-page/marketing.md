## Super powers this unlocks

- Names the three fixes most likely to move conversion.
- Returns verbatim current copy and the proposed replacement.
- Calls out what's already working so it doesn't get broken.
- Pairs each fix with an A/B test you can ship.

## How it works

Your Runneth pulls a fresh page summary, runs an eight-layer audit (message match, hero clarity, form friction, social proof, and the rest), and ranks every finding by expected lift times ease. The output is the top three plus an A/B queue.

## A real example

Reza ships an audit request on a customer's hero page on Tuesday. The audit comes back: the hero contradicts the ad headline, the form has two unnecessary fields, the testimonials sit below the fold. Each fix has the current copy, the replacement, and an A/B test ready to queue. He sends it to the customer. Two of the three are live by Friday.
