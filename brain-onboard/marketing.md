## Super powers this unlocks

- Skip the cold start. The brain knows your team, your brand, your customers, and your competitors from the first conversation.
- Every line is sourced. Every fact in the brain cites where it came from, so your team can trust it, edit it, or remove it.
- Builds and refreshes. As you turn on routines later, the brain refreshes automatically over time and only gets sharper.
- Inherits across the team. Whoever onboards first builds the most. Everyone after them inherits the work and adds their slice.

## How it works

Your CSM runs a brain prep on their side using everything Motion already knows about you, your conversations, your contacts, your reviews, your account. They send you a tar.gz file. You drop it into your Runneth and trigger brain-onboard. Runneth lays the package into your sandbox, runs live pulls against your Motion workspace and any tools you have connected like Yotpo or Shopify, scrapes the public web for organic posture, and synthesizes ten domains of brain content with source citations. When it finishes, you see a welcome card showing what each person on your team can now do.

## A real example

Bayley, a senior paid media manager at a DTC sleepwear brand, opens her team's Runneth for the first time after her CSM sends her brain package. Runneth shows her the team roster pulled from HubSpot, the brand's three customer personas with real review quotes, the seven competitors her team has named in past conversations, and the top creatives running in her account this month. She types "draft a brief for our spring drop." Runneth answers in the brand's voice, with the right persona and competitive frame. No setup, no explaining who they are. The whole team is operating against the same brain by the end of the week.
