## Super powers this unlocks

- Knows who's talking before they say a word.
- Members can only change their own space. Admins can change anything.
- New teammates get a home base on their first message, no setup needed.
- System files stay locked until an admin confirms each change.

## How it works

One skill, five phases. It writes a permission file, two identity resolvers, and a registry mapping every Slack ID and Motion email to a role. Admins get full write access. Everyone else stays inside their own space unless an admin approves something wider.

## A real example

A creative director at a DTC brand is rolling out Runneth to their whole performance team. She runs the skill, confirms she wants to be the first admin, and five minutes later her three media buyers each have their own personal space. When one of them tries to overwrite a shared creative brief the whole team uses, Runneth stops it and routes the request to the director to approve. The brief stays intact.
