# Conversation mission control

## What just opened up
Every Runneth conversation in this sandbox now shows up on a live kanban board. You can see what's active, what's waiting on your reply, and what's done at a glance, read the last message without opening the chat, and watch new activity land in real time on a 10-second poll.

## Try this now
1. **Open the board**: `Open conversation-manager.`
   _You'll get back:_ the app opens with three columns: Active, Waiting on me, Done. Every current conversation is grouped, last message previewed.
2. **Find what needs your reply**: `Which conversations are waiting on me right now?`
   _You'll get back:_ every conversation where Runneth has replied and is waiting on you, sorted by recency, with the last two lines of the agent message on each card.
3. **Find conversations that produced apps**: `Which of my conversations produced an app I can open?`
   _You'll get back:_ a list of conversations with app badges and a direct open link for each one.

## Compounds with
- **file-explorer:** Conversations and the files they produced live in the same browsable tree.
