## Super powers this unlocks

- Live kanban of every active conversation.
- Surfaces the ones waiting on your reply, in red.
- Detects when a conversation is finished, no manual archiving.
- Lets you read the last message inline.

## How it works

The app reads directly from the Runneth runtime database, so the board always reflects the real state of your conversations. Columns by status (waiting on you, in progress, done), color signals, and inline previews.

## A real example

Reza opens his laptop Monday morning with fourteen Runneth threads from the weekend. He pulls up Conversation Mission Control and three are in the waiting-on-me column. He replies to the two that matter and archives one. The other eleven are working away in the background. He has never had to click into a chat to find out whether it is done.
