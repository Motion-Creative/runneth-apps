⏰ Routines

The updates your team needs, delivered automatically.

Stop spending time manually pulling data and chasing down insights. Tell Runneth what to watch, when to report, and where to send it — and it handles the rest on its own.

Weekly creative performance read

Every Monday morning, Runneth pulls your top and bottom performers from the past 7 days, flags shifts in thumbstop and CTR, and posts a digest to your Slack channel — without anyone asking.

Winner alerts

Get notified the moment a creative crosses your spend threshold and beats benchmark on your primary KPI. Runneth flags it, summarizes why it's winning, and suggests what to iterate on next.

Weekly · Competitor intelligence

Monthly · Shareable report
