# Creative strategy

## What just opened up
You can now take any brand from a blank page to strategically mapped ads. Runneth builds the brand's creative foundation once (identity, products, voice of customer, audience language, competitors), then uses the creative strategy engine to decide what to say, then turns that into hooks, concepts, and format-ready briefs. Every piece is grounded in a specific person at a specific point in their journey, not generic best practices. The 15 skills are also callable on their own.

## Try this now
1. **Build the foundation for a brand**: `Build the creative strategy for [brand name + URL].`
   _You'll get back:_ the foundation layer runs and saves a per-brand bundle to `/agent/brain/creative-strategy/<workspace-slug>/`, then the engine maps your first messaging angles.
2. **Write hooks for a specific angle**: `Write hooks for [persona] at the problem-aware stage using a pain-agitation lens.`
   _You'll get back:_ hook-writing output grounded in the brand bundle, with tactic and voice-pattern variety.
3. **Reverse-engineer an ad**: `Break down this ad: [paste transcript + visuals, or upload the static].`
   _You'll get back:_ creative-analysis naming the hook, tactic, trigger, mechanic, and format, with what to test next.

## Compounds with
- **brand-audit:** if a brand already has a brand-audit bundle, the engine can read it as extra context.
- **creative-deep-dive:** deeper per-creative teardowns once ads are live.
- **paid-strategy-audit:** channel briefs that frame which angles to prioritize.
