# Video QA

## What just opened up
You now have a video review app where Runneth scores uploaded videos with timestamp-anchored feedback, and your accepts and rejects train a personal QA judge over time. Drop a video in, get a scored review with comments pinned to specific moments, accept or reject each call, and the next review is sharper.

## Try this now
1. **Upload a video and read the review**: `Open video-qa.`
   _You'll get back:_ the app opens; upload a video and Runneth posts timestamp-anchored comments, an overall verdict, and rubric scoring.
2. **Train the judge on a specific comment**: `On that video, reject the hook critique.`
   _You'll get back:_ the rejection logged, the rubric weighted away from that signal for your future reviews.
3. **Track training progress**: `How is the video QA judge evolving for me?`
   _You'll get back:_ rubric state, score-history trends, and the dimensions where the judge has shifted most.
