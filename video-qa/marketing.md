## Super powers this unlocks

- Pin comments to the exact second.
- Surfaces pacing, hook, and CTA issues automatically.
- Skips the issues you've rejected before.
- Learns your taste over time.

## How it works

Upload a cut to the app. Your Runneth watches it, drops timestamped comments where it spots issues, and asks you whether each call was right. Your responses train a personal judge stored in your brain.

## A real example

Josh's editor sends over a new 30-second cut on a Wednesday afternoon. Within a couple of minutes the app shows seven timestamps: a slow first three seconds, a missing CTA, a logo placement that fights the visual, and four nits. He accepts five, rejects two. The next cut from the same editor gets reviewed in three minutes, and the editor sees the same notes the strategist would have given.
